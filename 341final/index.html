    • As an artist, Haley is interested in religious iconography, specifically the religious icons in the worshipping of deities. Worship in these ceremonies can range from the consuming of a specific food to sacrificing an animal and using their blood for the worship rituals. Haley is also interested how expressionism uses color symbolically and optically to capture the mood of an artwork, such as red, which can function as both by communicating passion or aggression. Expressionism relies on distorting the forms and placing the focus on the mood and/or idea of the artwork.
