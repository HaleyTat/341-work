Blue Boy

The animation Blue Boy is an avant-garde animation appraoch to addressing the effects of man and the environment. Symbols of leadership in both realms can be seen through our human perspective as man at the top of the food chain. In nature and its ecosystems, there are many different creatures at the top of the food chain, one of them being the polar bear.
